What I do

My interests


  1. Elucidation of molecular mechanism of intracelluar signaling in response to oncogenic stress
    2. I am especially interested in the role of MAPK cascades as an initial sensor of mutations in the oncogenes.
  2. Development of a preventative medicine for ageing-related pathologies by targeting senecent cells in live tissues
    3. I am currently doing research of celluar biomarker that is specifically expressed in the cells experiencing oncogenic stress.
  3. Empowerment of younger female students
    4. I am working on a project called " GE at UTokyo" with my colleagues to raise awareness in gender equality on campus.
